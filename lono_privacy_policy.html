Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15th December 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how Lono by Blank Canvas Design Co. handles and protects your data. By using the app, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described below.

1. General Information

Lono is a Freemium app that enables users to create and edit photos and videos using a suite of creative tools, including presets, filters, and editing functionalities. The app stores all user-generated content (e.g., photos, videos, edits) exclusively on the user’s device.

Certain non-personal data is processed via third-party services to improve app performance and offer features such as analytics, stock content, and editing tools.

2. Data Collection and Transmission

We do not collect or store any personal data from users. However, Lono uses third-party services for specific purposes:

  • Firebase Analytics: Tracks app usage to enhance performance.
    https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Collects crash reports to help fix technical issues.
    https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Cloud: Provides thumbnails for previewing image presets.
    https://cloud.google.com/terms/data-processing-terms
  • Pexels: Supplies free stock images and videos for creative projects.
    https://www.pexels.com/privacy-policy/
  • Pixel SDK: Offers advanced photo and video editing features.
    https://pixelsdk.com/privacy

These services may collect non-personal data such as device type, operating system version, and app interaction logs.

3. Data Retention

All user-generated content (e.g., photos, videos) is stored locally on your device. We do not store or retain any data on external servers. Any data transmitted to third-party services (e.g., Firebase, Google Cloud) is used for real-time processing and not stored by us.

4. User Control and Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. Deleting the app from your device will permanently erase all associated data stored locally. We do not retain any of your data after the app is deleted.

5. Third-Party Data Processing

The app uses third-party services to enhance functionality. These services are governed by their respective privacy policies. We encourage you to review them for more details:

  • Firebase Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Cloud Privacy Policy: https://cloud.google.com/terms/data-processing-terms
  • Pexels Privacy Policy: https://www.pexels.com/privacy-policy/
  • Pixel SDK Privacy Policy: https://pixelsdk.com/privacy

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect data processed through the app. While we prioritize security, no method of transmission over the internet is entirely secure. We recommend enabling device security features, such as passcodes or biometric authentication, to safeguard your data.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. You will be notified of any significant updates through an app update. The latest version of the Privacy Policy will always be available in the app and on our website.
